# store
# mobile

The store feature allows you to validate dedicated docking areas where parcels are temporarily stored before delivery. This tool helps you identify and track which store a parcel belongs to for better operational visibility. Dispatchers and deliverers can use this to manage parcels effectively before they reach the customer.

### Getting Started

*   Access to the **Nomadia Delivery** mobile application.
*   A parcel with a valid barcode for scanning.

1. Open the application and navigate to the **Main Actions** menu.

![Frame at 0:34](../images/mobile-store_timestamp_0_to_34.png "Main Actions – The primary menu where the store feature is located.")

2. Tap the **Store** button.

![Flow 0:34 to 0:46](../images/mobile-store_timestamp_0_to_34_to_0_to_46.gif "Store – The transition from the main menu to the store setup screen.")

### Feature Overview

*   **Store**: Represents a dedicated docking area for temporary parcel storage.

![Frame at 0:46](../images/mobile-store_timestamp_0_to_46.png "Store – The interface used to define the storage location.")

*   **Barcode Scanner**: A tool used to scan parcel labels to link them to the assigned store.

![Frame at 1:15](../images/mobile-store_timestamp_1_to_15.png "Barcode Scanner – The icon that activates the device camera for scanning.")

*   **Storage Place**: A field in the back office where the validated store name is displayed for tracking.

### How To: Validate a Store

1. Navigate to the **Main Actions** menu.

![Frame at 0:34](../images/mobile-store_timestamp_0_to_34.png "Main Actions – Locating the start point for store validation.")

2. Tap **Store**.

![Flow 0:34 to 0:46](../images/mobile-store_timestamp_0_to_34_to_0_to_46.gif "Store – Selecting the store action from the menu.")

3. Define the **Store Name**.

![Frame at 0:53](../images/mobile-store_timestamp_0_to_53.png "Store Name – Inputting the specific name for the docking area.")

4. Tap on **Validate**.

![Flow 0:53 to 1:15](../images/mobile-store_timestamp_0_to_53_to_1_to_15.gif "Validate – Confirming the name entry to move to the scanning phase.")

5. Tap the **Barcode Scanner** to scan a parcel.

![Flow 1:15 to 1:21](../images/mobile-store_timestamp_1_to_15_to_1_to_21.gif "Barcode Scanner – The process of scanning a parcel label.")

6. Verify the **Store Name** is displayed on the screen.

![Frame at 1:30](../images/mobile-store_timestamp_1_to_30.png "Store Name – The system showing the associated store after a scan.")

7. Tap on the **Tick Mark**.

![Flow 1:30 to 1:35](../images/mobile-store_timestamp_1_to_30_to_1_to_35.gif "Tick Mark – Finalizing the selection of the scanned parcel.")

8. Tap on **Confirm** in the validation pop-up.

![Flow 1:39 to 1:43](../images/mobile-store_timestamp_1_to_39_to_1_to_43.gif "Confirm – The final step to save the store validation.")

### Productivity Tips

*   💡 **Operational Visibility**: Use the store validation feature to accurately track exactly which docking area a parcel is located in.
*   ⚠️ **Back Office Verification**: Always check the **Storage Place** field in the back office to ensure the store was saved successfully.
