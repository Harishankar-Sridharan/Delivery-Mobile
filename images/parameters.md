# parameters
# mobile

The **parameters** feature allows you to customize your application settings and view device information. Use this to adjust distance units, time formats, and language preferences to suit your workflow. It also provides tools to clear application data and verify synchronization status.

### Getting Started

*   Access to the Nomadia Delivery mobile application.
*   An active user account and valid login.

1. Open the **Main actions** menu.
2. Scroll down to the bottom of the list.
3. Tap **Parameters** to open the settings page.

![Flow 0:05 to 0:12](../images/mobile-parameters_timestamp_0_05_to_0_12.gif "Parameters Access – Scrolling down from main actions and tapping the parameters option.")

### Feature Overview

*   **Clear the catch**: Clears stored application data to resolve performance issues.
  
![Frame at 0:19](../images/mobile-parameters_timestamp_0_19_to_0_23.png "Clear the catch – The gear icon used to reset application data.")

*   **General conditions of use**: Displays the legal terms for using the application.
  
![Frame at 0:41](../images/mobile-parameters_timestamp_0_41_to_0_47.png "General conditions – Screen showing the general conditions of use.")

*   **Language**: Sets your preferred interface language.
  
![Frame at 0:52](../images/mobile-parameters_timestamp_0_52_to_0_57.png "Language selection – Menu to select the default application language.")

*   **Unit of measurement**: Switches distance displays between kilometers and miles.
  
![Frame at 1:01](../images/mobile-parameters_timestamp_1_01_to_1_06.png "Unit of measurement – Options for kilometer or millimeter measurement.")

*   **Time format**: Sets the clock display to 12-hour or 24-hour format.
  
![Frame at 1:08](../images/mobile-parameters_timestamp_1_08_to_1_13.png "Time format – Selection for 12 or 24 hour clock display.")

*   **Date format**: Choose between DDMMYY or MMDDYY formats.
  
![Frame at 1:15](../images/mobile-parameters_timestamp_1_15_to_1_19.png "Date format – Selection for day-month or month-day display.")

*   **About me**: Provides technical details like synchronization status and connection type.
  
![Frame at 1:28](../images/mobile-parameters_timestamp_1_28_to_1_55.png "About me – Detailed technical logs including user ID and synchronization status.")

### How To: Clear Application Cache

1. Tap the gear icon next to **Clear the catch**.
   
![Frame at 0:19](../images/mobile-parameters_timestamp_0_19_to_0_23.png "Clear the catch – Tapping the gear icon to initiate the reset.")

3. Review the confirmation pop-up.
   
![Frame at 0:23](../images/mobile-parameters_timestamp_0_23_to_0_26.png "Confirmation pop-up – Warning message asking to clear the application's catch.")

5. Tap **Confirm** to clear the catch.
   
![Flow 0:19 to 0:29](../images/mobile-parameters_timestamp_0_19_to_0_29.gif "Clear cache sequence – Confirming the action to clear stored application data.")

### How To: Change Unit Measurements

1. Navigate to the **My settings** section.
2. Tap **Unit of measurement**.
3. Select either **Kilometer** or **Milll**.
   
![Flow 1:01 to 1:06](../images/mobile-parameters_timestamp_1_01_to_1_06.gif "Unit selection – Changing the measurement unit in settings.")

### Productivity Tips

*   ⚠️ **Cache Confirmation**: You must tap **Confirm** on the pop-up for the application's catch to be cleared.
*   💡 **Connection Monitoring**: Use the **About me** section to check your **Wi-Fi** status and **synchronization status**.
