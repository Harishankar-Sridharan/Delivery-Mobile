# docking
# mobile

Docking prepares packages before loading them onto delivery routes. This feature ensures parcels are correctly assigned to a specific route for a particular day. It streamlines the logistics workflow by organizing inventory before it leaves the warehouse.

### Getting Started

*   Mobile device with the Nomadia Delivery app installed.
*   Access to the **Main Actions** menu.
*   Parcels with scannable barcodes.

1. Open the Nomadia Delivery app to the **Main Actions** screen.

![Frame at 0:08](../images/mobile-docking_timestamp_0_to_08.png "Main Actions – The main menu displaying various delivery and logistics tasks.")

2. Tap on **Docking**.

![Flow 0:08 to 0:17](../images/mobile-docking_timestamp_0_to_08_to_0_to_17.gif "Docking – Navigating from the main menu to the docking module.")

### Feature Overview

*   **Calendar Icon**: Use this to filter and select routes for a specific day.

![Frame at 0:21](../images/mobile-docking_timestamp_0_to_21.png "Calendar Icon – The icon used to open the date picker for route selection.")

*   **Barcode Scanner**: Activate this tool to scan parcel identifiers for docking.

![Frame at 1:01](../images/mobile-docking_timestamp_1_to_01.png "Barcode Scanner – The interface for scanning package barcodes.")

*   **Green Small Circle**: This indicator confirms that a parcel has been successfully scanned and docked.

![Frame at 1:21](../images/mobile-docking_timestamp_1_to_21.png "Green Small Circle – Visual confirmation that a parcel is ready for loading.")

*   **Tick Mark**: Tap this icon to finalize the scanning session for a route.

![Frame at 1:24](../images/mobile-docking_timestamp_1_to_24.png "Tick Mark – The button used to initiate the completion of route docking.")

*   **To Be Loaded Status**: Monitor this status in the back office to track progress.

![Frame at 1:55](../images/mobile-docking_timestamp_1_to_55.png "To Be Loaded Status – The dashboard view for verifying docked items.")

### How To: Dock Parcels for a Route

1. Tap on **Docking** from the main actions.

![Flow 0:08 to 0:17](../images/mobile-docking_timestamp_0_to_08_to_0_to_17.gif "Docking – Accessing the docking module from the main screen.")

2. Tap the **Calendar Icon** at the top right corner.

![Frame at 0:21](../images/mobile-docking_timestamp_0_to_21.png "Calendar Icon – Opening the date selection tool.")

3. Select the desired date and tap **OK**.

![Flow 0:21 to 0:38](../images/mobile-docking_timestamp_0_to_21_to_0_to_38.gif "Date Selection – Choosing a specific date to display relevant routes.")

4. Tap the specific **Route** you wish to process.

![Flow 0:42 to 1:01](../images/mobile-docking_timestamp_0_to_42_to_1_to_01.gif "Route Selection – Selecting a delivery route from the list.")

5. Tap the **Barcode Scanner** icon.

![Frame at 1:01](../images/mobile-docking_timestamp_1_to_01.png "Barcode Scanner – Activating the scanning interface.")

6. Scan the parcel barcodes.

![Flow 1:01 to 1:14](../images/mobile-docking_timestamp_1_to_01_to_1_to_14.gif "Scanning – The process of scanning barcodes to link parcels to a route.")

7. Verify the **Green Small Circle** appears on the right side of the parcel.

![Frame at 1:21](../images/mobile-docking_timestamp_1_to_21.png "Green Small Circle – Confirming the parcel is successfully scanned.")

8. Tap the **Tick Mark**.

![Frame at 1:24](../images/mobile-docking_timestamp_1_to_24.png "Tick Mark – Finishing the parcel scanning phase.")

9. Tap **Confirm** on the pop-up asking to complete the route docking.

![Flow 1:26 to 1:37](../images/mobile-docking_timestamp_1_to_26_to_1_to_37.gif "Confirm Popup – Finalizing the docking process for the selected route.")

### Productivity Tips

*   💡 **Back Office Verification**: View the docking status in the **Machine Logs** to confirm completion from the office.
*   ⚠️ **Status Checks**: Tap the **To Be Loaded Status** in the back office to ensure all items are accounted for.
