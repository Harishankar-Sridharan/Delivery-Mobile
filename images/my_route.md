# my_route
# mobile

The **My Roots** page provides drivers with access to all delivery routes currently assigned to their account. Users can track delivery progress, access navigation, and perform fulfillment tasks directly from their mobile device. This feature ensures real-time synchronization between the driver's actions and the back-office system.

### Getting Started

To access your assigned work, ensure you meet these requirements:
*   A valid **Nomadia Delivery** mobile account.
*   At least one route assigned to your profile.
*   Completed loading operations for the current route.

1. Open the **delivery mobile application**.

![Frame at 0:22](../images/mobile-my_route_timestamp_0_to_22.png "Delivery App – The application home screen is visible.")

2. Navigate to **My Roots**.

![Flow 0:22 to 0:32](../images/mobile-my_route_timestamp_0_to_22_to_0_to_32.gif "My Roots Navigation – The user navigates to the assigned routes list.")

### Feature Overview

*   **My Roots page**: Displays all routes currently assigned to the logged-in user.

![Frame at 0:27](../images/mobile-my_route_timestamp_0_to_27.png "My Roots page – A list of assigned routes for the driver is shown.")

*   **Fulfillment page**: Opens automatically if loading operations are already complete.

![Frame at 0:37](../images/mobile-my_route_timestamp_0_to_37.png "Fulfillment page – The active route's delivery screen is displayed.")

*   **Route Name**: Shows the identifier of the active route at the top of the screen.

![Frame at 1:09](../images/mobile-my_route_timestamp_1_to_09.png "Route Name – The name of the active route is visible above the progress bar.")

*   **Progress Bar**: Tracks current stops, completed stops, and remaining stops visually.

![Frame at 1:03](../images/mobile-my_route_timestamp_1_to_03.png "Progress Bar – Visual indicator of delivery status and stops.")

*   **Map View**: Provides a visual representation of the route sequence and machine locations.

![Frame at 0:53](../images/mobile-my_route_timestamp_0_to_53.png "Map View – The route map shows current position and delivery pins.")

*   **Three Dots**: Access operation activities like **Refresh My Tour** or **Optimize My Tour**.

![Frame at 1:15](../images/mobile-my_route_timestamp_1_to_15.png "Operation Menu – The three dots menu showing additional route actions.")

*   **Missions List**: Displays cards for each machine containing customer info and delivery status.

![Frame at 1:41](../images/mobile-my_route_timestamp_1_to_41.png "Missions List – Cards showing customer info and machine status.")

### How To: Interact with Customers

1. Locate the specific **Mission** card in your list.
2. Tap the **Phone Icon** to view the customer's phone number and call them.

![Frame at 2:31](../images/mobile-my_route_timestamp_2_to_31.png "Phone Icon – The button to call the customer from the mission screen.")

3. Tap the **Navigation Icon** to open **Google Maps** for GPS guidance.

![Frame at 2:45](../images/mobile-my_route_timestamp_2_to_45.png "Navigation Icon – The icon to launch GPS guidance to the customer.")

### How To: Perform a Delivery

1. Open the **Mission** card once you reach the destination.

![Frame at 3:00](../images/mobile-my_route_timestamp_3_to_00.png "Mission Detail – The specific machine task screen is opened.")

2. Tap the **Play Button** to begin the process.

![Frame at 3:05](../images/mobile-my_route_timestamp_3_to_05.png "Play Button – The action button to initiate the delivery process.")

3. Select **Delivery** and scan the parcel **QR code** or **Barcode**.

![Flow 3:14 to 3:22](../images/mobile-my_route_timestamp_3_to_14_to_3_to_22.gif "Parcel Scanning – The user scans the parcel to verify it.")

4. Long-press the delivery to select **Validation Without Scan** if manual entry is needed.

![Flow 3:38 to 3:43](../images/mobile-my_route_timestamp_3_to_38_to_3_to_43.gif "Manual Validation – Accessing the option to confirm delivery without scanning.")

5. Tap **Confirm** on the validation pop-up.
6. Choose a delivery option, such as **Dropped on the Doorstep**.

![Frame at 3:48](../images/mobile-my_route_timestamp_3_to_48.png "Drop-off Options – Selection screen for where the parcel was left.")

7. Tap the **Right Arrow** to capture a delivery photo.

![Flow 4:02 to 4:18](../images/mobile-my_route_timestamp_4_to_02_to_4_to_18.gif "Photo Capture – Sequence of taking and confirming a delivery photo.")

8. Tap the **Tick Mark** to collect the **Signature of the Recipient**.

![Frame at 4:25](../images/mobile-my_route_timestamp_4_to_25.png "Signature Screen – UI for capturing the customer's signature.")

9. Tap the **Next Arrow** to provide the **Signature of the Delivery Man**.
10. Tap **Confirm** to validate and sync the delivery with the back office.

![Flow 4:44 to 5:00](../images/mobile-my_route_timestamp_4_to_44_to_5_to_00.gif "Final Confirmation – Completing the delivery and triggering synchronization.")

### Productivity Tips

- 💡 **Automatic Redirection**: The app automatically moves you to the fulfillment page if loading is already done.
- 💡 **Visual Tracking**: Completed missions appear in green on the map and mission list for easy identification.
- 💡 **Live Sync**: All signatures and photos are immediately available in the back office once confirmed.
- ⚠️ **Mandatory Calls**: Calling the customer may be mandatory before continuing depending on your specific user rights.
