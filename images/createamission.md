# createamission
# mobile

Create pickup or delivery missions directly on mobile devices without using the back office. This feature allows drivers and operators to manage new assignments on the go. Mobile missions synchronize instantly with the back office for immediate processing.

### Getting Started

*   Active Nomadia Delivery mobile application.
*   Mobile device with a working camera for barcode scanning.
*   Proper permissions to access the mobile dashboard.

1. Open the **Nomadia Delivery** mobile app on your device.
2. Navigate to the mobile dashboard screen.

![Frame at 0:29](../images/mobile-createamission_timestamp_0_to_29.png "Mobile Dashboard – The main entry point for managing field operations.")

### How To: Create a Mission

1. Navigate to the **Quick Access** section on the mobile dashboard.

![Frame at 0:29](../images/mobile-createamission_timestamp_0_to_29.png "Quick Access – Dashboard area for navigation to creation features.")

2. Tap the **Create** button.

![Frame at 0:35](../images/mobile-createamission_timestamp_0_to_35.png "Create Button – Triggering the creation screen.")

3. Select **Pickup mission** or **Delivery mission** from the list.

![Frame at 0:49](../images/mobile-createamission_timestamp_0_to_49.png "Mission Selection – Choosing the specific type of mission to create.")

4. Tap **I understand** on the instruction popup.

![Frame at 1:02](../images/mobile-createamission_timestamp_1_to_02.png "Instruction Popup – Acknowledging how to provide label information.")

5. Tap the **QR code scanner** to scan a barcode or enter it manually.

![Flow 1:10 to 1:50](../images/mobile-createamission_timestamp_1_to_10_to_1_to_50.gif "Barcode Entry – Scanning or manually typing the package identification number.")

6. Set the mission location by selecting **Search for an address around me** or **My current location**.

![Frame at 2:14](../images/mobile-createamission_timestamp_2_to_14.png "Address Selection – Defining the mission location using GPS or search.")

7. Choose the **Type of container** and **Type of product**.

![Frame at 2:36](../images/mobile-createamission_timestamp_2_to_36.png "Product Categorization – Selecting the appropriate container and product types.")

8. Enter the package **Weight**, **Length**, **Width**, **Height**, and **Volume**.

![Flow 2:40 to 3:40](../images/mobile-createamission_timestamp_2_to_40_to_3_to_40.gif "Package Dimensions – Entering physical specifications for the mission.")

9. Tap the **Save** button.

![Frame at 3:43](../images/mobile-createamission_timestamp_3_to_43.png "Save Button – Storing the entered mission details.")

10. Tap the **Tick mark** to finish the information entry.

![Frame at 4:06](../images/mobile-createamission_timestamp_4_to_06.png "Completion Mark – Finalizing the mission data before creation.")

11. Tap **Confirm** on the final confirmation popup.

![Frame at 4:10](../images/mobile-createamission_timestamp_4_to_10.png "Confirm Creation – Submitting the mission to the system.")

### Productivity Tips

- 💡 **Manual Entry**: You can manually type a barcode if the camera cannot read a label.
- 💡 **Address Shortcuts**: Select a **Previous address** to quickly set locations for returning customers.
- 💡 **Instant Synchronization**: Created missions are immediately available to the back office for management.
