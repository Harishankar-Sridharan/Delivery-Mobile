# receipt
# mobile

**Nomadia Delivery** Receipt is used to acknowledge parcels arriving at a hub, warehouse, airport, or agency. This feature ensures all incoming shipments are correctly logged and updated in the system. Users can quickly process large volumes of incoming freight through scanning or manual status updates.

### Getting Started

*   A mobile device with the **Nomadia Delivery** app installed.
*   Required back-office configurations for sub-statuses.

1. Scroll down to the **Receipt** option in the main action menu.

![Frame at 0:15](../images/mobile-receipt_timestamp_0_to_15.png "Main Action Screen – The user scrolls down to find the receipt feature.")

2. Tap on **Receipt**.

![Frame at 0:17](../images/mobile-receipt_timestamp_0_to_17.png "Receipt Icon – The user selects the receipt button to open the feature.")

### Feature Overview

*   **Multiple Receptions Page**: Displays the current list of receptions available for processing.

![Frame at 0:23](../images/mobile-receipt_timestamp_0_to_23.png "Multiple Receptions Page – A list showing different reception records.")

*   **Barcode Scanner**: Uses the device camera to scan and identify parcels.

![Frame at 0:30](../images/mobile-receipt_timestamp_0_to_30.png "Barcode Scanner – The scanning interface for processing parcels.")

*   **Tick Mark**: Initiates the final confirmation process for the selected parcels.

![Frame at 0:49](../images/mobile-receipt_timestamp_0_to_49.png "Tick Mark – The button used to complete the current batch.")

*   **Confirm**: Finalizes the status change and finishes the receiving process in the pop-up window.

![Frame at 0:58](../images/mobile-receipt_timestamp_0_to_58.png "Confirm Pop-up – The dialogue box asking to finish receiving scanned packages.")

*   **Sub Status**: Provides specific reasons for the receipt status based on company configuration.

![Frame at 1:35](../images/mobile-receipt_timestamp_1_to_35.png "Sub Status Menu – A list of configurable reasons for the parcel status.")

### How To: Scan a Parcel for Receipt

1. Select any reception from the list.

![Frame at 0:26](../images/mobile-receipt_timestamp_0_to_26.png "Reception Selection – Selecting a specific reception entry.")

2. Tap the **Barcode Scanner**.

![Frame at 0:30](../images/mobile-receipt_timestamp_0_to_30.png "Barcode Scanner – Activating the camera to scan parcels.")

3. Scan the parcel barcode to change its status.

![Flow 0:30 to 0:38](../images/mobile-receipt_timestamp_0_to_30_to_0_to_38.gif "Scanning Flow – Scanning a parcel to change its status.")

4. Select the scanned parcel in the list.

![Frame at 0:43](../images/mobile-receipt_timestamp_0_to_43.png "Parcel Selection – Choosing the scanned parcel for confirmation.")

5. Tap on the **Tick Mark**.

![Frame at 0:49](../images/mobile-receipt_timestamp_0_to_49.png "Tick Mark – Finishing the scanned package process.")

6. Tap **Confirm** on the pop-up stating "Are you sure you want to finish...".

![Frame at 0:58](../images/mobile-receipt_timestamp_0_to_58.png "Confirm Action – Confirming the reception of scanned packages.")

### How To: Update Status Manually

1. Long press the parcel you wish to update.

![Flow 1:13 to 1:23](../images/mobile-receipt_timestamp_1_to_13_to_1_to_23.gif "Long Press Flow – Accessing manual status updates for a specific parcel.")

2. Toggle the **Received** status.

![Frame at 1:31](../images/mobile-receipt_timestamp_1_to_31.png "Received Toggle – Switching the status to received.")

3. Toggle the specific **Sub Status** required (e.g., "Drop the hub").

![Frame at 1:40](../images/mobile-receipt_timestamp_1_to_40.png "Sub Status Toggle – Selecting a specific receiving reason.")

4. Tap the **Tick Mark**.

![Frame at 1:55](../images/mobile-receipt_timestamp_1_to_55.png "Tick Mark – Initiating the manual update confirmation.")

5. Tap **Confirm** on the confirmation pop-up.

![Frame at 2:14](../images/mobile-receipt_timestamp_2_to_14.png "Confirm Action – Confirming the manual status change.")

### How To: Record Damaged Parcels

1. Long press the target parcel.
2. Tap on **Not Received**.

![Frame at 2:25](../images/mobile-receipt_timestamp_2_to_25.png "Not Received Button – Selecting the option for items not acknowledged.")

3. Toggle a reason such as **Box Damaged** or **Product Damaged**.

![Frame at 2:35](../images/mobile-receipt_timestamp_2_to_35.png "Damage Reason – Selecting the specific reason for non-receipt.")

4. Tap the **Tick Mark**.

![Frame at 2:43](../images/mobile-receipt_timestamp_2_to_43.png "Tick Mark – Processing the non-receipt status.")

5. Tap **Confirm** to finish the process.

![Frame at 2:53](../images/mobile-receipt_timestamp_2_to_53.png "Confirm Action – Finalizing the damaged status update.")

### Productivity Tips

*   💡 **Back Office Monitoring**: View all reception updates and machine logs in the back office immediately after confirmation.
*   ⚠️ **Configuration Limits**: Sub-statuses are limited to what has been defined in your back-office configuration.
