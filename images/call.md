# call
# mobile
The contact management feature allows you to access and update your list of essential phone numbers. It ensures quick access to key contacts while you are in the field.

### Getting Started
*   Mobile device with **Nomadia Delivery** app installed.
*   Active user account.
1. Open the application to the **Main Action Screen**.

### Feature Overview
*   **Call Icon**: Opens the contact list for quick communication.

![Frame at 0:10](../images/mobile-call_timestamp_0_to_10.png "Call Icon – The call icon button on the main action screen.")

*   **Contact Page**: Displays all previously configured phone numbers.

![Frame at 0:21](../images/mobile-call_timestamp_0_to_21.png "Contact Page – A list showing existing contact names and numbers.")

*   **Plus Icon**: Opens the form to create a new contact.

![Frame at 0:27](../images/mobile-call_timestamp_0_to_27.png "Plus Icon – The add button at the bottom of the contact page.")

*   **Add Button**: Saves the new contact details to your list.

![Frame at 1:04](../images/mobile-call_timestamp_1_to_04.png "Add Button – The confirmation button to save contact details.")

*   **X Icon**: Returns you to the main interface.

![Frame at 1:25](../images/mobile-call_timestamp_1_to_25.png "X Icon – The close button used to exit the contact list.")

### How To: Add a New Contact
1. Tap the **Call Icon** on the **Main Action Screen**.

![Flow 0:00 to 0:10](../images/mobile-call_timestamp_0_to_00_to_0_to_10.gif "Main Action Screen – Tapping the call icon to open contacts.")

2. Tap the **Plus Icon** at the bottom of the screen.

![Flow 0:12 to 0:27](../images/mobile-call_timestamp_0_to_12_to_0_to_27.gif "Contact Page – Accessing the new contact entry form.")

3. Enter the contact name in the **Name** box.
4. Enter the phone number in the **Phone Number** box.

![Flow 0:30 to 0:46](../images/mobile-call_timestamp_0_to_30_to_0_to_46.gif "New Contact Page – Entering a name and phone number for a new contact.")

5. Tap **Add** to save the information.

![Frame at 1:04](../images/mobile-call_timestamp_1_to_04.png "Contact List – The new contact appearing in the list.")

6. Tap the **X Icon** at the bottom to return to the **Main Action Screen**.

![Flow 1:20 to 1:25](../images/mobile-call_timestamp_1_to_20_to_1_to_25.gif "Contact List – Exiting the contacts view and returning to the main menu.")

### Productivity Tips
- 💡 **Quick Access**: Configured numbers allow for fast communication with important contacts.
- 💡 **Future Use**: Saved contacts remain in your list for all future tasks.
