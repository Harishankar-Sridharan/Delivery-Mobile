# ratetheapp
# mobile

The Rate the App feature allows you to share feedback on your experience with Nomadia Delivery directly through the app. By providing a star rating, you help improve the platform's performance and usability for all users.

### Getting Started

*   Nomadia Delivery app installed on a mobile device.
*   An active Google Play Store account.
*   Stable internet connection.

1. Open the Nomadia Delivery app to the **Main Actions** screen.

    ![Frame at 0:05](../images/mobile-ratetheapp_timestamp_0_to_05.png "Main Actions – The primary menu screen containing app settings and features.")

2. Scroll down to the bottom of the menu.

    ![Flow 0:05 to 0:10](../images/mobile-ratetheapp_timestamp_0_to_05_to_0_to_10.gif "Scroll Down – Navigating to the bottom of the actions list.")

### Feature Overview

*   **Rate the App feature**: Redirects you to the official Google Play store page for feedback.
    
    ![Frame at 0:14](../images/mobile-ratetheapp_timestamp_0_to_14.png "Rate the App button – The specific menu item used to initiate the rating process.")

*   **Star Rating**: Displays a scale from 1 to 5 to evaluate the application.

    ![Frame at 0:25](../images/mobile-ratetheapp_timestamp_0_to_25.png "Rating scale – Stars used to select a score between 1 and 5.")

*   **Post**: Submits your chosen star rating to the store.

    ![Frame at 0:31](../images/mobile-ratetheapp_timestamp_0_to_31.png "Post button – The action to finalize and upload your review.")

*   **Review More**: Opens a detailed view of existing ratings and feedback.

    ![Frame at 0:50](../images/mobile-ratetheapp_timestamp_0_to_50.png "Review More – Link to see other user ratings and comments.")

### How To: Rate the App

1. Tap **Rate the App feature** from the main actions list.

    ![Flow 0:10 to 0:19](../images/mobile-ratetheapp_timestamp_0_to_10_to_0_to_19.gif "Redirection – Transitioning from the Nomadia Delivery app to the Google Play Store.")

2. Scroll down on the **Google Play Nomadia Delivery** page to find the rating section.
3. Select a star rating from 1 to 5.

    ![Frame at 0:25](../images/mobile-ratetheapp_timestamp_0_to_25.png "Star selection – Choosing the desired score for the app.")

4. Tap **Post** to submit your feedback.

    ![Frame at 0:39](../images/mobile-ratetheapp_timestamp_0_to_39.png "Post – Finalizing the submission of the rating.")

5. Tap **Done** to exit the rating screen.

    ![Frame at 0:50](../images/mobile-ratetheapp_timestamp_0_to_50.png "Done button – Closing the Google Play interface after posting.")

### How To: View App Reviews

1. Tap **Review More** after submitting your rating.

    ![Frame at 0:50](../images/mobile-ratetheapp_timestamp_0_to_50.png "Review More button – Accessing the detailed review history section.")

2. Review the displayed ratings from other users.
3. Tap **Done** to complete the process.

    ![Frame at 0:55](../images/mobile-ratetheapp_timestamp_0_to_55.png "Final Done – Finishing the review session and returning to the app.")

### Productivity Tips

- 💡 **Instant Update**: Your latest rating is displayed immediately after you tap the post button.
- 💡 **Feedback Check**: Use the review more option to stay informed about other users' experiences and tips.
