# update_missions
# mobile

The update missions feature allows users to modify mission information by scanning parcel barcodes directly on a mobile device. This streamlines the data entry process and ensures accurate records for dispatchers and planners. Users can quickly verify and save mission updates to keep the logistics system current.

### Getting Started

*   Mobile device with a functioning camera for scanning.
*   Authorized access to the **Nomadia Delivery** mobile application.

1. Open the mobile application and locate the **Main actions** menu.

![Frame at 0:07](../images/mobile-update_missions_timestamp_0_to_07.png "Main actions – The user menu containing primary mission management options.")

2. Scroll down to find the **Update missions** option.

![Flow 0:07 to 0:12](../images/mobile-update_missions_timestamp_0_to_07_to_0_to_12.gif "Main actions – Scrolling through the menu to locate the Update missions button.")

### Feature Overview

*   **Barcode scanner icon**: Launches the device camera to read parcel barcodes for mission identification.

![Frame at 0:18](../images/mobile-update_missions_timestamp_0_to_18.png "Update missions page – A blank page featuring the barcode scanner icon for parcel entry.")

*   **Tick mark**: Confirms the scanned item and proceeds to the detailed information page.

![Frame at 0:30](../images/mobile-update_missions_timestamp_0_to_30.png "Confirmation screen – The tick mark used to validate a scan.")

*   **Save**: Submits all changes made to the mission details to the database.

![Frame at 1:14](../images/mobile-update_missions_timestamp_1_to_14.png "Details page – The save button used to finalize mission updates.")

*   **Edit icon**: Located in the back office to view and verify the successful mission update.

![Frame at 1:29](../images/mobile-update_missions_timestamp_1_to_29.png "Back office – The edit icon used to view the updated mission details.")

### How To: Update a Mission

1. Tap on **Update missions** from the **Main actions** screen.

![Flow 0:07 to 0:12](../images/mobile-update_missions_timestamp_0_to_07_to_0_to_12.gif "Navigation – Moving from main actions to the update missions screen.")

2. Tap the **Barcode scanner icon** to scan the parcel.

![Flow 0:18 to 0:25](../images/mobile-update_missions_timestamp_0_to_18_to_0_to_25.gif "Scanning – Activating the camera to scan a parcel barcode.")

3. Tap the **Tick mark** to display the details page.

![Flow 0:30 to 0:37](../images/mobile-update_missions_timestamp_0_to_30_to_0_to_37.gif "Scan Confirmation – Transitioning from the scanner to the details entry page.")

4. Enter the details that you want to update.

![Flow 0:37 to 0:55](../images/mobile-update_missions_timestamp_0_to_37_to_0_to_55.gif "Details Page – Entering required information for the mission update.")

5. Tap on **Save** once you enter the required details.

![Flow 0:55 to 1:14](../images/mobile-update_missions_timestamp_0_to_55_to_1_to_14.gif "Saving – Clicking save to complete the update process.")

### Productivity Tips

- 💡 **Verification**: View the updated mission page in the back office by tapping the **Edit icon** to confirm changes.
