# supervision
# mobile

The Supervision feature provides real-time visibility into your delivery operations directly from your mobile device. Dispatchers can track route progress, monitor customer deliveries, and use geographic map views to ensure efficiency.

### Getting Started

*   Active Nomadia Delivery mobile application.
*   Assigned driver routes and machine collection tasks.

1. Scroll down the list of main actions in the app.
   
![Frame at 0:06](../images/mobile-supervision_timestamp_0_to_06.png "Main Actions – The user scrolls through the main menu to locate the supervision feature.")

3. Tap the **Supervision** button to open the dashboard.
   
![Frame at 0:13](../images/mobile-supervision_timestamp_0_to_13.png "Supervision Button – The user taps the Supervision icon to access live tracking data.")

### Feature Overview

*   **Tracking**: Displays the total percentage of completed routes and active machines for the current period.
  
![Frame at 0:22](../images/mobile-supervision_timestamp_0_to_22.png "Tracking Page – This screen shows high-level progress bars and machine counts.")

*   **Roads**: Lists individual routes including customer names, deliverer assignments, and pending hours.
  
![Frame at 0:39](../images/mobile-supervision_timestamp_0_to_39.png "Roads Tab – Detailed breakdown of specific routes and assigned personnel.")

*   **Map**: Visualizes customer locations and delivery points on an interactive map.
  
![Frame at 1:09](../images/mobile-supervision_timestamp_1_to_09.png "Map View – A geographic representation of all customer stops for the day.")

*   **Filter**: Provides options to refine the dashboard view based on priority, start order, or search terms.
  
![Frame at 1:16](../images/mobile-supervision_timestamp_1_to_16.png "Filter Menu – The button at the top right opens the customization panel.")

### How To: Monitor Real-Time Progress

1. Open the **Supervision** page and select the **Tracking** tab.
   
![Frame at 0:22](../images/mobile-supervision_timestamp_0_to_22.png "Tracking View – The user enters the main supervision dashboard.")

3. Check the percentage of routes completed at the top of the screen.
   
![Frame at 0:31](../images/mobile-supervision_timestamp_0_to_31.png "Progress Metrics – The dashboard highlights the percentage of finished tasks.")

5. Scroll down to see specific machine collection statuses for each route.
   
![Flow 0:31 to 0:39](../images/mobile-supervision_timestamp_0_to_31_to_0_to_39.gif "Scrolling Metrics – The user scrolls to see detailed machine status lists.")

### How To: View Route Details

1. Tap the **Roads** tab from the main Supervision screen.
   
![Frame at 0:39](../images/mobile-supervision_timestamp_0_to_39.png "Roads Selection – Switching to the detailed list view of all active routes.")

3. Review customer names and assigned deliverers for each entry.
   
![Frame at 0:51](../images/mobile-supervision_timestamp_0_to_51.png "Route Details – The screen displays specific customer and driver pairings.")

5. Refresh the page to display the most recent data from the field.
   
![Flow 0:59 to 1:07](../images/mobile-supervision_timestamp_0_to_59_to_1_to_07.gif "Refreshing Roads – The user triggers a data update to get the latest status.")

### How To: Filter and Search the Dashboard

1. Tap the **Map** tab to view customer locations.
   
![Frame at 1:09](../images/mobile-supervision_timestamp_1_to_09.png "Map Tab – Selecting the geographic overview of deliveries.")

3. Tap the **Filter** icon in the top right corner.
   
![Frame at 1:16](../images/mobile-supervision_timestamp_1_to_16.png "Filter Access – Opening the settings to narrow down the displayed routes.")

5. Select **Yes** or **No** under **Priority to Late Routes**.
   
![Frame at 1:25](../images/mobile-supervision_timestamp_1_to_25.png "Priority Toggle – Choosing whether to prioritize late routes in the view.")

7. Choose **Less Advanced** or **More Advanced** in the **Route Start Order** section.
   
![Frame at 1:40](../images/mobile-supervision_timestamp_1_to_40.png "Order Selection – Refining the sorting order for the route list.")

9. Enter a delivery band or route name in the **Search** box.
    
![Frame at 1:48](../images/mobile-supervision_timestamp_1_to_48.png "Search Function – Filtering the results by typing specific route identifiers.")

11. Tap **Apply** to save your filter selections.
    
![Flow 1:48 to 1:57](../images/mobile-supervision_timestamp_1_to_48_to_1_to_57.gif "Applying Filters – Confirming settings to update the dashboard view.")

### Productivity Tips

*   💡 **Real-Time Accuracy**: Use the refresh function frequently to ensure you are seeing the latest data from your deliverers.
*   💡 **Fast Navigation**: Search by delivery band or road name to find specific information without scrolling through long lists.
