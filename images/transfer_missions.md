# transfer_missions
# mobile

Reassign machines between deliverers instantly using numeric delivery. This feature allows dispatchers to manage route changes and operational issues efficiently. You will achieve a seamless transfer of delivery tasks confirmed in your system.

### Getting Started

*   Active Nomadia Delivery mobile account.
*   Mobile device with a functioning camera for scanning.
*   Access to the **Main actions menu**.

1. Open the Nomadia Delivery app on your mobile device.
2. Access the **Main actions menu**.

![Frame at 0:21](../images/mobile-transfer_missions_timestamp_0_to_21.png "Main actions menu – The primary navigation interface for reassignment tasks.")

### Feature Overview

*   **transfer machines**: Initiates the process to move a delivery task from one user to another.

![Frame at 0:21](../images/mobile-transfer_missions_timestamp_0_to_21.png "transfer machines – The specific menu option for starting a mission transfer.")

*   **machine's detail**: Displays specific task information to ensure the correct machine is selected for transfer.

![Frame at 0:43](../images/mobile-transfer_missions_timestamp_0_to_43.png "machine's detail – Information screen used for task verification.")

*   **barcode scanner**: Uses the device camera to identify user profiles and machines.

![Frame at 1:22](../images/mobile-transfer_missions_timestamp_1_to_22.png "barcode scanner – The interface for capturing machine and profile IDs.")

*   **I understand**: A confirmation button that acknowledges the scanning instructions.

![Frame at 1:15](../images/mobile-transfer_missions_timestamp_1_to_15.png "I understand – The button used to dismiss the instructional popup.")

### How To: Transfer Machines

1. Open the **Main actions menu**.
2. Tap **transfer machines**.

![Flow 0:00 to 0:21](../images/mobile-transfer_missions_timestamp_0_to_00_to_0_to_21.gif "transfer machines – Navigating from the home screen to the transfer tool.")

3. Choose the machine you want to transfer.

![Frame at 0:36](../images/mobile-transfer_missions_timestamp_0_to_36.png "Machine Selection – Selecting the specific unit for reassignment.")

4. Review the **machine's detail** to ensure accuracy.

![Frame at 0:43](../images/mobile-transfer_missions_timestamp_0_to_43.png "machine's detail – Verifying the machine information before the scan.")

5. Read the popup regarding user profile scanning.
6. Tap **I understand**.

![Frame at 1:15](../images/mobile-transfer_missions_timestamp_1_to_15.png "I understand – Confirming the scanning workflow.")

7. Tap the **barcode scanner**.

![Frame at 1:15](../images/mobile-transfer_missions_timestamp_1_to_15.png "barcode scanner – Activating the camera tool.")

8. Scan the user's profile barcode.
9. Scan the machines you would like to transfer.

![Flow 1:15 to 1:32](../images/mobile-transfer_missions_timestamp_1_to_15_to_1_to_32.gif "Scanning Sequence – The process of scanning the profile followed by the machines.")

10. Confirm the transferred mission appears in the **back office**.

![Frame at 1:36](../images/mobile-transfer_missions_timestamp_1_to_36.png "back office – Final confirmation of the reassigned task in the management portal.")

### Productivity Tips

*   💡 **Operational Agility**: Use this feature to handle sudden route changes or issues without manual paperwork.
*   ⚠️ **Accuracy Check**: Always review the machine details before scanning to prevent transferring the wrong delivery task.
